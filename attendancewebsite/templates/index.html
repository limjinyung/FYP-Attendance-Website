Monash Attendance System


MAS is a user-friendly, effective and powerful system that provides smoothly integrated automated access control, attendance, reporting and more.

Student will be able to access and view their weekly performance in every unit and download their own attendance data.

Staff will have access to student's attendance data and will be abe to download data in .csv format. Also, MAS is able to analyze student's attendance by considering a few factors like weather, class time and clubs.

Please login as student or staff to have different access to the system.
